# Anchors

Anchors protect your fasting streak when life gets in the way.

Instead of losing months of progress because of a single missed day, Anchors provide a limited way to preserve your streak.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='what-are-anchors'>
  What are Anchors?
</h2>

Anchors are consumable streak protection items.

If you miss a fasting session while an Anchor is available, one Anchor is automatically consumed and your streak continues uninterrupted.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='earning-anchors'>
  Earning Anchors
</h2>

Anchors are earned by consistently completing fasting sessions.

Regular fasting gradually rewards additional Anchors over time.

Because Anchors are limited, they should be viewed as an emergency resource rather than something to rely on regularly.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='using-anchors'>
  Using Anchors
</h2>

Anchors activate automatically when necessary.

No manual action is required.

If an Anchor is available, your streak is preserved.

If no Anchors remain, missing a fast ends your current streak.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='checking-your-anchor-count'>
  Checking your Anchor count
</h2>

Your available Anchors are displayed on the Gamification page.

This allows you to understand how much streak protection remains.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='best-practices'>
  Best practices
</h2>

Try to avoid depending on Anchors.

They're intended for unexpected situations such as:

- illness
- travel
- family events
- emergencies

Building consistent habits is always more valuable than preserving a streak at any cost.
