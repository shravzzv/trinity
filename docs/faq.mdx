# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most commonly asked questions about Trinity.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='does-trinity-work-offline'>
  Does Trinity work offline?
</h2>

Yes.

Trinity is designed as an offline-first Progressive Web App.

Most features continue working without an internet connection.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='do-i-need-an-account'>
  Do I need an account?
</h2>

No.

You can begin using Trinity immediately without creating an account.

Future versions may offer optional synchronization features.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='will-i-lose-my-data'>
  Will I lose my data?
</h2>

Your data is stored locally on your device.

Future updates may introduce backup and export functionality for additional peace of mind.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='can-i-edit-my-fasts'>
  Can I edit completed fasts?
</h2>

Yes.

Completed fasting sessions can be edited if you accidentally recorded incorrect information.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='can-i-delete-fasts'>
  Can I delete fasting sessions?
</h2>

Yes.

You remain in control of your fasting history.

Deleting a fast updates your statistics accordingly.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='how-do-i-install-trinity'>
  How do I install Trinity?
</h2>

Open Trinity in a supported browser and choose **Install** or **Add to Home Screen**.

See the **Getting Started** guide for detailed instructions.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='does-trinity-contain-ads'>
  Does Trinity contain advertisements?
</h2>

No.

Trinity is designed to provide a clean, distraction-free experience.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='does-the-timer-keep-running'>
  Does the timer keep running if i close the app?
</h2>

Yes.

Your fasting timer continues tracking elapsed time even after the application is closed.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='where-can-i-report-bugs'>
  Where can I report bugs?
</h2>

Bug reporting information will be available through the project's GitHub repository.

Feedback is always appreciated.
