# Statistics

Statistics help you understand your long-term fasting habits.

Rather than focusing on a single day, Trinity summarizes your progress over weeks and months.

---

## Overview

The Statistics page provides an overview of your fasting activity.

Information updates automatically as you complete new fasting sessions.

---

## Current streak

Your current streak represents the number of consecutive successful fasting sessions.

Maintaining a streak encourages consistency over time.

---

## Fasting history

Every completed fast is recorded in your history.

Your history allows you to review:

- completed sessions
- missed sessions
- fasting durations
- completion dates

---

## Weekly and monthly progress

Progress summaries help identify long-term patterns.

Instead of judging individual days, use these views to understand your overall consistency.

---

## Charts

Visual charts make trends easier to understand.

As your history grows, charts become increasingly useful for identifying improvements over time.

---

## Using statistics effectively

Statistics are intended to encourage reflection rather than perfection.

A missed day is rarely important.

Improving your consistency over months is what matters most.
