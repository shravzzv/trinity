# Timer

The Timer is the heart of Trinity.

It tracks your fasting sessions, records your progress, and provides a simple view of how much time remains in your current fast.

---

## Starting a fast

To begin fasting:

1. Open the **[home](/home)** page.
2. Select a fasting plan.
3. Press **Start Fast**.

Once started, the timer begins counting immediately.

You may safely leave the application at any time. Trinity continues tracking your fast in the background.

---

## During a fast

While fasting, the Timer displays:

- Remaining time
- Selected fasting plan
- Progress indicator
- Current streak status

Your progress updates automatically without requiring manual refreshes.

---

## Ending a fast

When you've finished fasting:

1. Return to Trinity.
2. Press **End Fasting**.

The completed fasting session is automatically added to your history and contributes toward your statistics, streaks, and experience.

---

## Editing a fast

Mistakes happen.

If you accidentally started or ended a fast at the wrong time, Trinity allows you to edit the recorded fasting session.

Editing a fast updates your history and recalculates any affected statistics.

---

## Missed fasts

Missing a fasting session doesn't erase your progress.

Depending on your available Anchors and streak status, Trinity may preserve your current streak.

See the **[Anchors](/docs/anchors)** guide to learn more.

---

## Tips

- Choose a fasting schedule you can consistently maintain.
- Avoid frequently changing plans.
- Focus on long-term consistency rather than perfect completion.
- Check your history occasionally to review your progress.
