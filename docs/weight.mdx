# Weight

The Weight page allows you to record and review changes in body weight over time.

Consistent measurements help you understand long-term trends rather than day-to-day fluctuations.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='adding-a-weight-entry'>
  Adding a weight entry
</h2>

To record your weight:

1. Open the **Weight** page.
2. Select **Add Weight**.
3. Enter your current weight.
4. Save the entry.

Your new measurement immediately appears in your history.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='editing-weight-entries'>
  Editing weight entries
</h2>

Incorrect measurements can be edited later.

Updating an entry automatically refreshes any charts or summaries.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='weight-history'>
  Weight history
</h2>

Every recorded measurement is stored chronologically.

Reviewing your history makes it easier to understand how your body changes over time.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='weight-chart'>
  Weight chart
</h2>

The weight chart visualizes your progress.

Because body weight naturally fluctuates, trends over weeks are usually more meaningful than changes from one day to the next.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='best-practices'>
  Best practices
</h2>

For more consistent measurements:

- Weigh yourself at approximately the same time each day.
- Use the same scale whenever possible.
- Wear similar clothing.
- Focus on long-term trends rather than individual measurements.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='privacy'>
  Privacy
</h2>

Your weight data belongs to you.

Trinity stores your measurements so you can review your progress over time without unnecessary distractions or advertisements.
