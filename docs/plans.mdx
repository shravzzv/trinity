# Plans

Plans allow you to reuse your preferred fasting schedules.

Instead of entering fasting durations every day, simply create a plan once and reuse it whenever needed.

---

## Creating a plan

To create a plan:

1. Open the **[Home](/home)** page.
2. Click **Select Plan**.
3. Choose a fasting duration.
4. Save the plan.

Your new plan becomes available immediately.

---

## Editing a plan

Plans can be modified at any time.

Changes only affect future fasting sessions.

Previously completed fasts remain unchanged.

---

## Recommended plans

Many users begin with:

- 12:12
- 14:10
- 16:8
- 18:6
- 20:4
- 23:1

Choose a schedule that matches your lifestyle rather than the most difficult option.
