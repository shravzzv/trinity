# Plans

Plans allow you to reuse your preferred fasting schedules.

Instead of entering fasting durations every day, simply create a plan once and reuse it whenever needed.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='creating-a-plan'>
  Creating a plan
</h2>

To create a plan:

1. Open the **Plans** page.
2. Select **New Plan**.
3. Enter a name.
4. Choose a fasting duration.
5. Save the plan.

Your new plan becomes available immediately.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='editing-a-plan'>
  Editing a plan
</h2>

Plans can be modified at any time.

You may change:

- Name
- Fasting duration
- Default settings

Changes only affect future fasting sessions.

Previously completed fasts remain unchanged.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='using-a-plan'>
  Using a plan
</h2>

When starting a fast, simply select one of your existing plans.

The timer automatically applies the plan's configured duration.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='recommended-plans'>
  Recommended plans
</h2>

Many users begin with:

- 12:12
- 14:10
- 16:8
- 18:6
- 20:4

Choose a schedule that matches your lifestyle rather than the most difficult option.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='managing-plans'>
  Managing plans
</h2>

Plans can be:

- edited
- duplicated
- deleted

Removing a plan never deletes completed fasting history.

---

<h2 className='scroll-mt-32' id='tips'>
  Tips
</h2>

Keep only a small number of plans.

Most users only need two or three schedules for everyday use.
